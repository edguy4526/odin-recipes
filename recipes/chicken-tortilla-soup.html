Chicken Tortilla Soup

shamelessly sourced from Allrecipes back to main page

If you're looking for a hearty and filling soup that will satisfy your Mexican food craving, then look no further. This chicken tortilla soup is the perfect comfort food that offers both spicy, Mexican-flair and hearty ingredients that will keep you full and happy.

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time: 25 mins
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (10.5 oz) can condensed chicken broth
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 (15 oz) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 large boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup whole corn kernels, cooked
  • 1 cup white hominy
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup crushed tortilla chips
  • 2 medium avocados, sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped green onions

Directions

Step 1

Heat oil in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; saute until soft. Stir in crushed tomatoes, condensed broth, water, chili powder, and oregano; bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.

Step 2

Stir in black beans, coocked chicken, corn, hominy, chile peppers, and cilantro. Simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 3

Ladle soup into individual bowls; top with tortilla chips, avocado slices, Monterey Jack cheese, and onions.
