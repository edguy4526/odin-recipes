Homemade Mac and Cheese

shamelessly sourced from Allrecipes back to main page

Skip the boxed stuff and make homemade mac and cheese tonight. This from-scratch macaroni and cheese recipe will become a staple in your dinner rotation.

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 45 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • Box of macaroni noodles, uncooked
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Whole milk
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt, pepper, and paprika
  • Bread crumbs

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; saute until soft. Stir in crushed tomatoes, condensed broth, water, chili powder, and oregano; bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in black beans, coocked chicken, corn, hominy, chile peppers, and cilantro. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Ladle soup into individual bowls; top with tortilla chips, avocado slices, Monterey Jack cheese, and onions.
